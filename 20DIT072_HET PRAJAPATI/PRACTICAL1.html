Charusat University

Charotar University of Science and Technology is established under the Gujarat Act No. 8 of 1995, Government of Gujarat. University Grants Commission has empowered CHARUSAT to award Degrees under Section 22 of UGC Act 1956. Earlier campus was known as Education Campus Changa until 2009.
